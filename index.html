QR Tracking and Monitoring

The system will implement QR codes as the main selling feature, allowing iCargo partners and partnerâ€™s customers to easily make new shipments and track their packages via smartphone in real-time.

Printing of Waybill

The system will generate a shipment tracking number, which the customer can use to track their package. This number is also printed on the shipping label, so when a customer requests a pickup package, the courier can scan it to update the shipment status.

Pricing Quotation

The system provides a quotation that gives a customer an idea of the possible total price depending on the sender and recipient location provided.

Payment Integration

System allows customers to choose different payment
methods including Cash on Delivery, Credit Card, and
E-wallet. Payment using credit cards and e-wallets is
integrated with the use of Paymongo API, which provides
access to or control over the user's experience and integrates payments directly with the system and database.

Advance Freight Forwarding

Freight forwarding is the strategic logistics planning and
execution for the international transportation of commodities
on behalf of logistic companies. A freight forwarder will, among
other things, negotiate freight rates, track cargos, file customs paperwork, and consolidate freight.
